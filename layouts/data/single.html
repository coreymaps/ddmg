DDMG produces satellite-derived damage assessment datasets using Sentinel-1 SAR coherent change detection. These datasets are made available for research, humanitarian, and journalistic use under a CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license.

Each dataset includes damage classification rasters (Cloud-Optimized GeoTIFF) and damage vector boundaries (GeoJSON). See the accompanying metadata for methodological details, thresholds, and temporal coverage.

Available Datasets

File Formats

  • Damage raster (.tif) — Cloud-Optimized GeoTIFF with 6 bands: damage mask, z-score, pre-conflict mean coherence, pre-conflict std, conflict mean coherence, valid-for-monitoring mask.
  • Damage vectors (.geojson) — Polygonized damage footprints with per-feature attributes.
